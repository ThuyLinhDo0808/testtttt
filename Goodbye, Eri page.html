Goodbye, Eri

Paperback – June 27, 2023

by Tatsuki Fujimoto (Author)


An explosive story from the mad genius behind Chainsaw Man and Look Back!


Young filmmaker Yuta contemplates suicide after the death of his mother, but a chance meeting with a mysterious girl will alter his life in an explosive way.


From the mad genius behind Chainsaw Man and Look Back comes a new story about coping with loss.


Yuta’s moviemaking career started with a request from his mother to record her final moments. After her death, Yuta meets a mysterious girl named Eri, who takes his life in new directions. The two begin creating a movie together, but Eri is harboring an explosive secret.

One of the best oneshot I read

Fujimoto never miss for me, since I'm also a big cinema fan. It's clear throughout his works that cinema and movies play a big role in his storytelling, and this oneshot is the most direct showcase of that. I think it is a really nice gift too, given it's a oneshot but is decently long.

Kyouta

A beautiful story

Goodbye, Eri was the second story from Tatsuki Fujimoto I read after going through Chainsaw Man, and I would say there isn't many other stories that touched my heart like it did. I have reread it numerous times, and each time I find myself falling in love with it more and more, and the ending never fails to make me cry and tug on my heartstrings.

Highly recommend!

I went into this book blind and I definitely recommend doing the same. It was a quick read but it was a very interesting way of storytelling that had me hooked the entire time. This isn't your usual plot and I think that's what makes it so engaging.
